What are Solamonsters?

Solamonsters are said to have originated from the mysterious Monsterverse.

Based on current understandings, there are 4 different generations of Solamonsters that exist. However, little is known about them beyond that.

Rumors are that they are a friendly yet territorial bunch. In large groups, there's nothing they can't accomplish...

Project Components

The Bounty

○ A bounty of up to 2 SOL has been placed on every Solamonster.

○ If a bounty is claimed, the returned Solamonster will be sent back to the Monsterverse - never to be seen again on the Solana blockchain.


Solamonsters DAO

○ All generations of Solamonsters will assemble together to form a DAO focused on defi, NFT, and metaverse investments.

○ Upon the full release of NEONEXUS, the Solamonsters DAO will work to establish and operate The Solamonsters District to generate revenue for holders.


NFT Merging

○ Solamonsters from the same generation will be able to be merged together to create a custom Solamonster.

○ Holders of multiple Solamonsters from the same generation will be able to choose attributes from their existing NFTs to merge.

FAQ

What is The Solamonster Society?

The Solamonsters Society is a deflationary collection of 10,072 NFTs consisting of 5 generations of 2000 Solamonsters. The purpose of this project is to create a long-lasting community that provides value to every Solamonster.

What is the bounty?

There will be a bounty of up to 2 SOL placed on every Solamonster, which can be claimed immediately after mint. To claim the bounty, minters must send in their Solamonsters, thus decreasing the total supply.

What is Solamonsters DAO?

Value is provided to holders through exclusive access to a DAO focused on metaverse investments in NEONEXUS. The Solamonsters DAO will work to create The Solamonsters District in game once NEONEXUS launches. All DAO members will receive a share of all revenue generated within The Solamonsters District.

What is The Solamonsters District?

The Solamonsters District will be establish in NEONEXUS and will consist of 6 sectors. The aim of The Solamonsters District is to create the best community in NEONEXUS, as well as to generate revenue for holders.

What are the Executive NFTs?

There will be 12 Executive NFTs dropped through auction. Holders of these NFTs will gain direct access to the project team, manage The Solamonsters District, and receive the most exclusive benefits.

When does minting start?

The Executives Drop will be released in January 2022. The Generational Drops will released in late January to February 2022. Any updates will be posted to Twitter and Discord.

What is the mint price?

The Executives Drop will start at 1 SOL. The mint price for the Generational Drops will be 0.5 SOL.

Is there a rarity ranking?

The official rarity ranking will be available post mint in the Rarity tab.
