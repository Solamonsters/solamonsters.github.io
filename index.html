The Solamonsters Society

The Solamonsters Society is a deflationary collection initially consisting of 9,636 NFTs on the Solana blockchain.

The collection includes 12 Executive NFTs, 8,888 Solamonster NFTs, and airdrop/special event NFTs. Every NFT earns the holder a bi-weekly dividend.

Additionally, there is a bounty of up to 2.22 SOL placed on every Solamonster NFT minted!

FAQ

What is The Solamonster Society?

The Solamonsters Society is a deflationary collection of 9,636 NFTs, which includes 8,888 Solamonsters. The purpose of this project is to create a long-lasting community and provide value to every Solamonster through recurring bi-weekly dividends and access to exclusive DAO benefits.

What is the bounty?

There will be a bounty of up to 2.22 SOL placed on every Solamonster, which can be claimed immediately after mint. To claim the bounty, minters must send in their Solamonsters, thus decreasing the total supply.

What is the Solamonsters DAO?

Value is provided to holders through exclusive access to an investment DAO focused on DeFi and NFT assets that are income-producing. All holders will receive bi-weekly dividends from the generated revenue as well as exclusive access to furthur project features. The goal for the DAO is to be the best community possible on Solana.

What are the Executive NFTs?

There will be 12 Executive NFTs dropped through auction. Holders of these NFTs will be airdropped 4 Executive Solamonster NFTs, gain direct access to the project team to help manage the DAO, receive 15 bi-weekly dividend shares, and many other exclusive benefits.

When does minting start?

The Executives Drop will be released in early February 2022. The Solamonster Drops will released in late February 2022. Any updates will be posted to Twitter and Discord.

What is the mint price?

The Executives Drop auction will start at 1 SOL. The mint price for the Solamonster Drops will be tiered:

Ventus - 0.55 SOL
Ignis - 0.66 SOL
Terra - 0.77 SOL
Aquae - 0.88 SOL

Is there a rarity ranking?

The official rarity ranking will be available post mint in the Rarity tab.
